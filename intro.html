Introduction to coffee

This module ideal for anyone who is new to the coffee industry or just has an interest in this wonderful drink.

Overview

This course charts coffee’s journey from its origins in Ethiopia to the major commodity it is today, enjoyed by millions of people around the world.

From farming the cherries through drying, roasting, brewing and finally drinking, this module looks at the processes coffee goes through.

This course also includes “coffee cupping which allows you to taste the various flavours coffee has to offer.

It has only one level and brings 10 points towards the Coffee Diploma.
