Coffees

We carefully source and choose our coffee offerings to represent the uniqueness of each origin.

Then, we roast and blend based on two main philosophies: to preserve and highlight the natural characteristics of the original bean and to ensure that a cup of Coffee Project Co. beans coffee will always taste as good as it smells.

Single Origin

This is where the fun is. These are seasonal coffees that are constantly changing. Often we buy entire micro lots, giving us world exclusivity on that coffee – when we do this you know that the coffee is truly exceptional.

Blends
