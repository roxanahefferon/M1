Barista SCA

SCA Points

This course is at foundation level and brings 5 points towards the Coffee Diploma.

New to coffee? This one day class focusses on the core skills required to deliver all the key drinks on the menu.

You’ll also gain an insight into how coffee is grown, processed and roasted.

Overview

Explore the differences between Arabicas and Robustas and how to maintain freshness.

Extract the perfect espresso following a set of steps that make up the industry discipline

Steam different milk textures to bring together a whole host of espresso based drinks.

Round off the learning with machine cleaning and basic maintenance, before sitting a series of assessments.

Experience

No prior knowledge required

Cost

EUR 240 including exam, Cert Fee (EUR 60) + VAT
