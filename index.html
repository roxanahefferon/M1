our courses

SCA Courses

The SCA Barista Courses and Coffee Skills Programme is an education system aimed to meet the needs of baristas and coffee professionals everywhere. SCA is a controlled and certified education system with a curriculum written and updated by the best coffee experts in the coffee industry.

SCA Barista Courses and Coffee Skills Program allows the coffee professionals to complete coffee/barista courses in six subject modules and three levels of certifications, amassing course credits each time a certification is successfully passed that count towards the 100 points needed to gain SCA internationally recognized Coffee Diploma.

It’s your choice on the SCA coffee/barista modules you take to get you to 100 points. SCA Certification is based on both technical knowledge and practical examination.
