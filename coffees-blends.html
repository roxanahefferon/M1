Blends

Project Co. Blend

Our signature blend. A great, full body with slightly heavier texture and deep, dark brown colour. You’ll recognize it by its full, sweet aroma.

Red Rock

This is our 3 bean North Italian blend. Containing the only speciality graded Robusta in the world, this blend gives very full body and sweet flavour, without the cheap iconic Robusta flavour anticipated by the coffee varietal.

Moka Java Blend

This Indonesian blend was created to offer our organic interpretation of one of the first blended coffees. Sweet tropical fruit mingles in the cup with hints of spice and floral tones. Crisp, balanced acidity gives this coffee a clean, but balanced cup.

French Roast

A traditional French Roast blend roasted slightly lighter, resulting in a coffee with a rich, smoky taste and a semi-sweet finish.

Breakfast Blend

This remarkable blend offers the best of all worlds. With powerful flavour, full finish, and hearty aroma, it’s the perfect way to start the day off right!
